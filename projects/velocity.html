Velocity by Adidas Anticipation

May - August 2017
Prototype shipped, currently in beta

Interaction Design Mobility and iOT Artificial Intelligence

BRIEF

To understand the behavior and needs of employees in Munich’s emerging tech scene and creating an experience to promote mobility, healthy living and an enjoyable work-life balance.

This project is a collaboration between Adidas Group, and BMW Innovation Group, with the aim to create a B2B2C mobility service which would launch in Munich, Germany in January 2018.

This project is currently being implemented in beta mode.

GETTING STARTED

Understanding current market and technology trends in sports and lifestyle habits

I conducted secondary research in three spaces that pertained to health, stakeholders involved, market and technology trends. The trends which stood out initially, grouped by category, were:

  • EAT (trends in sustainability, availability of healthy foods at scale, and motivations leading to certain choices)
  • MOVE(trends in mobility, commuter behaviors, and pain points associated with transportation options)
  • MIND(trends in digital solutions for mindfulness, triggers for stress, and iOT solutions for biometric tracking)

Our researched helped us narrow into the MOVE space and we focused on the following mobility framework to guide our designs:

Our project focuses on giving employees of large organizations an entry point to promoting and maintaining a healthy lifestyle

REFLECTIONS

Be collaborative, work fast

I had a hugely rewarding experience during my internship. I was the only female on the team, the only interaction designer, and I was involved in every stage of the process from the initial brief (Adidas x BMW Partnership), user research, synthesis, prototyping and user testing of the prototypes. I was also involved in the product management aspect of the project, closely co-creating with the engineers and project managers to create a strategy and roadmap for the product, which I really enjoyed.
